           The fields on the Billing Analysis screen are:

            Field              Description
            Technician The name of the technician.

            Work               The number of work orders the technician has worked
            Orders             on.

            Segments The number of segments associated with the selected
                     technician.

            Parts              The total amount (in dollars) of parts sold on the work
                               orders the selected technician worked on.

            Labor              The total amount of labor hours charged to the work
                               orders the selected technician worked on.

            Other              Any other miscellaneous charges associated with the
                               work orders worked on by the selected technician.

            Total              The total amount of charges for work orders worked
                               on by the selected technician.


           Click on a Technician to see a breakdown of the technician's
           assigned work orders.

           Click the Total button to open the Totals screen, which displays
           totals of the fields that appear on the Billing Analysis screen.
