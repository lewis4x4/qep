          The fields on the Prospect Board screen:

           Field                            Description
            Salesperson                     The name of the salesperson associated
                                            with the prospect.

            Customer/Status The customer name and status of the
                            sale/prospect.

            Selling/Trading                 The unit being sold to the customer and
                                            any units being traded in by the customer.

            Added/Modified                  The date the prospect was created, added
                                            or modified.

            Source                          The source that the prospect originates
                                            from.


          Comments added in the Comments field on the Prospect
          screen appear on a second line under the Customer/Status
          field.

          Click the Need to create a new prospect? Click here to add
          link to add a prospect.

          Click the John Deere Quote Upload link to upload a JD Quote II
          quote/ potential purchase order using the John Deere Quote
          Upload screen. The John Deere Quote Upload link only
          appears if your dealership has been licensed to use the
          JD Quote II interface and the Access JDQuote Upload switch is
          activated on the Authorization List for Product Support -
          Prospect Board screen. See About JD Quote II.

          To edit an existing prospect, click a Salesperson name to open
          the Prospect screen.

          Click a customer name in the Customer/Status field to open the
          Customer Profile screen.
