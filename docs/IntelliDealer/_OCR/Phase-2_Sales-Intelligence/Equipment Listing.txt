          Searching Equipment Profiles

                    Use the search bar to search keywords or if you know
                    specific search terms such as a serial or stock number on
                    an equipment unit.

                    By default, the search bar finds your text wherever it
                    appears—even in the middle of a word. You don’t need to
                    type the full word.

                    When you type multiple words separated by spaces in the
                    search bar, the search finds results that contain any of
                    those. Each one is matched wherever it appears—even in
                    the middle of another word.

                    You can search by equipment stock number, identifier,
                    base, make, model, serial number, options, factory, group,
                    customer number or fleet number, salesperson, or supplier
                    invoice number. However, searching by location while using
                    the search bar is not supported.

                    When the Filter option is turned on (it is on by default),
                    the search uses all of the filters together. Results must
                    match every filter you enter—not just one.

                    To keep specific search fields open or closed by default
                    use the Sticky feature.

                    If Options is selected, executing a search also scans
                    Equipment Base & Options tab for a matching option.

                    If Options is selected and the Or checkbox is selected, the
                    IntelliDealer searches for any one of the options to match,
                    otherwise it searches for all the options to match.
