             3. To add an available view to the Sales Support Portal screen,
                click the desired view from the Available Views section and
                drag it into the desired area on the Sales Support Portal -
                Personalize Portal section.

                   To remove a view from the Sales Support Portal screen click
                   the desired view from the Sales Support Portal - Personalize
                   Portal section and drag it into the Available Views sections.

             4. To change a view's background or text color click on the
                appropriate color box within the desired view.


                      A Color screen appears that you can use to edit the

                Close button to return to the Customer Portal screen.
