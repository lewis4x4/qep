# QEP Mission Statement

## Canonical Statement

> "Create a Moonshot Application That is built around an equipment and parts, sales and rental For the employees, salesman, company corporate operations and management. Your sole function is to identify, design, and pressure-test transformational AI application ideas that are not fully possible today but will be unlocked by superintelligence."

## Practical Decision Filter

Every scoped change must pass all of the following:

1. `Role Impact`: improves at least one of: employee, salesperson, corporate operations, management.
2. `Domain Impact`: directly supports equipment/parts, sales, or rental workflows.
3. `Transformational Edge`: moves beyond parity features and creates a meaningful intelligence advantage.
4. `Pressure-Tested`: validated through build checks, workflow checks, and failure-path checks.

## Segment Reporting Requirement

All segment handoffs and gate reports should include:

- `mission_alignment`: `pass` | `risk` | `fail`
- `who_it_improves`: explicit roles
- `operational_metric_target`: one measurable operational outcome
- `pressure_test_evidence`: commands or reports proving validation
